HOW TO PLAY

Overview

Royalty High is a 2-player strategy card game. Deploy units to your board, attack your opponent's board, and protect your Royals. The player with the most Royal points at the end wins.

The Cards

Each game has a random mix of Royals (J=1pt, Q=2pts, K=3pts) that always totals 12 points.

Setup

Each player draws 5 cards. You may mulligan once to discard your hand and draw 5 new cards. On your first turn, you do not draw a card.

Your Turn

Draw 1 card from the deck (except your first turn), then choose a path:

  • Path A (default): Take 1 action
  • Discard 2, Put 2: Discard 2 cards, then take 2 actions

Actions

  • Deploy: Place cards of the same rank face-down on the left or right side of your board. If your board has fewer than 3 units, you may keep placing more cards as part of the same action.
  • Attack: Send cards of the same rank to attack the opponent's board from the left or right side. This always ends your turn.
  • Pick up discard: Take the top card of the discard pile (costs 2 actions).
  • Pass: Do nothing with your remaining action.

Units

Multiple cards of the same rank deployed together form a single unit. Their power stacks (e.g., two 5s = power 10).

Attacking

  • Your attacker fights defenders from the chosen side (left or right) one at a time.
  • If attacker power > defender power, the defender is destroyed and leftover power carries through to the next defender.
  • If attacker power < defender power, the attack is blocked. Attacker is destroyed, defender survives.
  • If equal, both are destroyed.
  • Royal attackers destroy one unit (regardless of power), then are destroyed themselves.
  • Attacking a Royal defender: Both attacker and Royal are destroyed.

Ace (Special)

When an Ace is defending and is attacked by a non-Royal, the attacker replaces the Ace on the board. The Ace is destroyed.

Hand Limit

Maximum hand size is 10 cards. If your hand exceeds 10 at the end of your turn, you must discard down to 10.

End of Game

When the deck runs out, players must attack each turn with whatever cards they have. When both players have no cards left, the game ends.

All board units are revealed and surviving Royals are scored:

RoyalPoints
Jack (J)1 point
Queen (Q)2 points
King (K)3 points

The player with the most Royal points wins.
